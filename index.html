About Us

Heritage Utsav is the reformed, annual cultural and technical festival of Heritage Institute of Technology, Kolkata - one of the finest education institutions in the state. It combines the realms of technology and culture into 6 days of fun and ecstacy. with events ranging from varied fields - dance, music, theatre, fine arts, sciecne, technology, debating, photography, flim and literature. The highlights of the festival are usually the DJ and Guest Performances that span over the last two evenings. The festivals the gathering of talents from all aforesaid fields, from al over the city in paticular and the country in general.
With a plethora of cultural, sports and technical events and inter-college competitions, the 16th anniversary of the fest, Heritage Utsav 2019 is touted to be one of the best college fests of Eastern India. Gear up for a major happening from the April 1st to 6th, 2019.
