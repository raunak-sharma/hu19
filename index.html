Heritage Utsav '19

April 1, 2019

About Us

Heritage Utsav is the annual cultural fest of Heritage Institute of Technology, Kolkata and is held every year in March/April. This is organised by Students' Council of Heritage Institute Of Technology. With one of the biggest artist line-up ever this year, Heritage Utsav 2019 is touted to be one of the best college fests of Eastern India. With over 40 cultural and sports events and an overall footfall of 40,000 students spanning 3 days of the fest, Heritage Utsav is something you will be looking forward to. 1st April - 6th April are the dates one must book for a fun filled ride of inter-college competitions and great performances by imminent artists.
